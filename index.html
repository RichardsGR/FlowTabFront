Features

Everything you need to master your tabs

FlowTab combines the speed of Spotlight with browser tab intelligence to eliminate tab clutter forever.

Global Hotkey

Press Ctrl+K from any app to bring up the floating search window. It appears instantly, always on top.

Fuzzy Search + Pinyin

Find any page by name, URL, or pinyin. Powered by nucleo, the same fuzzy-matching engine used in modern code editors.

Smart Routing

Automatically detects if a URL is already open. Switch to the existing tab instead of creating duplicates — save memory, stay focused.

Bookmark Import

One-click import from Chrome or Edge bookmarks. Your existing bookmark hierarchy is automatically flattened and deduplicated.

History Analysis

Analyzes your browsing history across Chrome, Edge, Arc, and Brave to surface your most-visited sites. Top 50 frequent pages at a glance.

Smart Dedup + Theme

Intelligent URL normalization catches duplicates (www vs non-www, trailing slashes). Beautiful frosted glass UI with automatic light/dark mode.

How It Works

Three steps to tab freedom

1

Press Ctrl+K

Summon the floating search window from anywhere — your IDE, browser, terminal, or Finder. No need to switch context.

2

Search & Find

Type a few characters to fuzzy-search across your indexed pages, open tabs, and browser history. Pinyin support for Chinese users.

3

Jump Instantly

Hit Enter. FlowTab intelligently switches to an open tab or creates a new one. Zero friction, zero duplicates.
